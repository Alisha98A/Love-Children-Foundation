An estimated 153 million children worldwide are orphans

Love Children Foundation is a non-profit funding organization. We have been up and running since 2011 where we helped hundreds of homeless kids, to find their meaning and purpose in life. With the help of our wonderful staff and volunteers, we offer them education, food and shelter.
