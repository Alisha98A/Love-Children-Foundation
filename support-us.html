There are many ways to support us

Our organisation would not survive if it weren't for the help from people in form of fund raising, volunteering and partnership. All the money goes directly to the children, which means we do not take anything in our own pockets. This way to ensure that you can be safe by choosing US.

We have many staff and volunteers that daily comes up with new ideas of how we can do more for these children, and we're very proud of that

Donate

All donations goes directly to Love Children Foundations, we use the money wisely to provide food, shelter and clothes for our children. The more donations we get, the more can we help. It doesn't matter how much you can give, wheter it's 10 dollar or 100 dollar. We're grateful no matter what!

Volunteer

We offer volunteering for those who are interested in Love Children Foundation and want to learn more about our culture and how we take care of children. As a volunteer you get to help with all kinds of activities, which is beneficial both for you and our children. Being a volunteer is something beautiful, and when you come back home. You will take this experience with you for the rest of your life. If you're interested, just fill in your contact information in our contact form on this site, and we will reply with further information.

Partnership

Interested in becoming a partner? We collaborate with many companies and if you think you can help with school supplies or fundraising. Feel free to reach out to us for more information.
