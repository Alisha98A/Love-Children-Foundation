Our Story

The grounder of this foundation is Elena Wattson, 36 years old living in the UK. The idea came from when she was traveling with her parents as a kid to meet children at orphanages in Thailand. Seeing many kids stuggle made her heartbroken, and since then, she always had a thought of helping kids in the future. 2011, she went together with her parents to raise money to give to homeless children in Africa. Today Love Children Foundation have helped more than 1200 kids.

Vision

Our vision is to help as many children as we can, by building new orphanages around Africa. We have place for more. But it's not just that we want to help as many as possible, we want these children to turn into well well educated adults, happy and driven. Therefore, we put a lot of focus in education through different projects where we can see early on, what each individual is good at & can develop in. Our goal is to help these people become independent, so that they can shine and grow.
