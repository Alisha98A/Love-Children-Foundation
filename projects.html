Our Projects

We have many projects ongoing and are constantly looking for developing new tools and ideas. We belive that people learn best by doing.

Photography

One of our projects is teaching how to become a photographer. In the beginning, children get to play around with it for fun. From there, we hold classes in Photography every week. It's not only good for development, but they also learn how to see the beauty of everything. It could be anything from people to nature. But most importantly, children think it's fun!

Education

The most important thing is education. Therefore, we help them get to school everyday so that they can become something in the future. In school they get to learn many things, from how to read to basic knowledge of history, maths, biology, chemistry etc.

After school, staff & volunteers do everything they can to help these children with homework and other duties.

Sports

We at Love Children Foundation believe that physical activity is important for children to help them grow best way possible. We provide dance lessons for those who wants to dance, and football for those who want to play football. This is highly appreciated by the kids!
